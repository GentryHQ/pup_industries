Our Vision

Our vision is to save the planet from plastic pollution by creating value and wealth through plastic recycling, while promoting a more sustainable and prosperous future for all.

Our Mission

Our goal is to establish a trusted Nigerian brand that leverages innovation to transform plastic waste into value, providing exceptional customer experiences along the way.

Our Values

PUP stands for Plastic Upcycled Products. It is a social enterprise created with a goal to accelerate plastic upcycling.

What We Do

At our company, we upcycle plastic waste into practical products that are both functional and environmentally friendly

Source Plastics

We source out our plastics waste from local communities. We sort, clean and get them ready for production.

Production

These plastic are taken to our production team and they are turned into useful products such as the shoe polish and plastic boards.

Distribute & Sell

The products are now ready for sale and distribution to our various outlets and store within the country.

Our Products

Transforming Waste into Innovation: Explore our product section and discover a range of innovative offerings that transform waste into inspiration.

PUP Shoe Polish

PUP Shoe Polish is a pioneering product that upcycles plastics into a high-quality shoe polish, providing a sustainable and effective way to utilize plastic waste while maintaining the shine and care of footwear.

PUP Boards

Plastic Boards is a transformative solution that upcycles plastic materials into durable and versatile boards, offering an eco-friendly alternative to traditional wood-based products while reducing plastic waste.

P2P - Plastic to Paint

P2P (Plastics to Paints) is an innovative process that upcycles plastic waste into high-quality paints, reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainable resource utilization.

Our Impacts

Transforming plastic waste into positive impact: Our efforts are reducing waste, promoting sustainability, and creating economic opportunities in local communities

PUP Clubs
