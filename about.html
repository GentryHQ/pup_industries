ABOUT US

At PUP Industries, we are a trailblazing social enterprise on a mission to accelerate plastic upcycling and pave the way towards a sustainable future. With a strong commitment to environmental stewardship and innovation, we have discovered an innovative way to create value from used plastics by transforming them into useful products under our brand, Plastic Upcycled Products (PUP).

Our relentless dedication to addressing the global plastic waste crisis has led us to develop ground-breaking techniques that harness the potential of plastic upcycling. By reimagining waste as a valuable resource, we turn discarded plastics into high-quality, functional, and eco-friendly products that make a positive impact. One of our flagship offerings is PUP Shoe Polish, a testament to our expertise in converting plastic waste into durable, reliable, and strong solutions. This exceptional shoe polish not only restores and enhances the appearance of footwear but also embodies our vision of promoting circularity and sustainability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to save the planet from plastic pollution by creating value and wealth through plastic recycling, while promoting a more sustainable and prosperous future for all.

Our Mission

Our goal is to establish a trusted Nigerian brand that leverages innovation to transform plastic waste into value, providing exceptional customer experiences along the way.
